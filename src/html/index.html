April - September, 2022

clusterfuck.

In April we called a plumber to fix our leaky bathroom sink.

Fast forward a few days to the plumbers cutting a hole in the kitchen floor to access the bathroom plumbing, only to find that all of the plumbing was bullshit and falling apart.

The shower drain was not connected to anything.

The shower had been draining into the ground 🙃

renovation.

They had to redo the plumbing of the entire bathroom and kitchen. When they went to install the toilet, they found that the entire subfloor had rotted through.

We would need to renovate the entire bathroom

porta potty.

We rented a porta potty put it in the alley behind our house.

This was our bathroom for 2 months while we looked for a contractor.

contractor.

Our contractor let us know that the bathroom reno would spread into the kitchen. Their tools would take up the rest of the kitchen, the dining room, and the office.

Our house would be unlivable for at least 8 weeks.

house sitting.

Our friend was going out of town for all of July, so we moved into their gorgeous house.

Despite our awesome digs, the stress of not being able to be “home” started bringing out the worst of my partner and I.

We noticed that Lucy (my black lab mix) was losing her appetite, probably due to stress.

airbnb.

At the end of July, our friend returned and we moved into an airbnb. A $4000 airbnb, which was somehow the cheapest we could find.

Dogs were allowed, but only non-shedding breeds, so Lucy had to go stay with my parents. I went over every day to feed her and take her on walks.

lucy.

I got a vet appointment to check on Lucy’s dwindling appetite.

On August 17th, around 3:30pm, I found out that my dog was dying.
