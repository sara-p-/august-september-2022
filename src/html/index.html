August/September 2022

The past few months have been a clusterfuck.

In April we called a plumber to fix our leaky bathroom sink.

Fast forward a few days to the plumbers cutting a hole in the kitchen floor to access the bathroom plumbing, only to find that all of the plumbing was bullshit and falling apart.

The shower drain was not connected to anything.

It was draining into the ground. 🙃

The plumbers had to redo the plumbing of the entire bathroom.

When they went to install the toilet, they found that the entire subfloor had rotted through.

We would need renovate the entire bathroom.

We went 2 months without a toilet while we found a contractor.

We rented a porta potty and had it put in the alley behind our house.

The contractor we found was a godsend.

Trustworthy and experienced.

He let us know that the bathroom reno would spread into the kitchen. Their tools would take up the rest of the kitchen, the dining room, and the office.

Our house would be unlivable for at least 8 weeks.
